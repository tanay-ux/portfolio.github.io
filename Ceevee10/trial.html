About Me

Heya, I'm Tanay and I'm a User Experience designer from Symbiosis Institute of Design, Pune. I've been freelancing on multiple projects including award winning short films and stop motion animation videos. I understand people's needs and work to design and redesign applications and websites to make them more user friendly. I have a minimalist sense of design and prefer clean and functional designing. I'm also an avid sports player, cyclist and work as a photographer in my free time.
